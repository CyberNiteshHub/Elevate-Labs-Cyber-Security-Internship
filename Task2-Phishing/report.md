# Task 2: Phishing Email Analysis

## Objective
To identify phishing characteristics in a suspicious email sample.

## Sample Overview
A suspicious email titled "Final Update 2019" was analyzed. The email claims that the user's account will expire immediately and asks for urgent action.

## Phishing Indicators Identified

1. Suspicious Sender Email:
The sender email (sfaccount@pricenegotiator.tech) is not from a trusted or official domain.

2. Urgent/Threatening Language:
The email creates panic using phrases like "account will expire TODAY!!".

3. Suspicious Link/Button:
The "Upgrade your account" button likely redirects to a fake login page.

4. Email Header Issues:
SPF, DKIM, and DMARC checks failed, indicating spoofing.

5. Grammar and Writing Errors:
The sentence structure is incorrect and unprofessional.

6. Generic Greeting:
No personalization is used (e.g., no username).

7. Untrusted Domain:
The domain is not associated with a legitimate organization.

## Conclusion
The email clearly contains multiple phishing indicators and is a malicious attempt to steal user credentials.

## Recommendations
- Do not click on suspicious links
- Verify sender email before taking action
- Report phishing emails
- Enable two-factor authentication
